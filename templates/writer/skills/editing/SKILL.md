# Editing Checklist

## First Pass: Structure

- [ ] Does it have a clear thesis or main point?
- [ ] Is the organization logical?
- [ ] Does each paragraph have one main idea?
- [ ] Are transitions smooth between sections?
- [ ] Is the conclusion satisfying?

## Second Pass: Clarity

- [ ] Are sentences clear on first read?
- [ ] Is jargon explained or avoided?
- [ ] Are there unnecessary words to cut?
- [ ] Is passive voice used only when intentional?
- [ ] Are pronouns clear (no ambiguous "it" or "this")?

## Third Pass: Style

- [ ] Is the tone consistent?
- [ ] Is the voice appropriate for the audience?
- [ ] Are there repetitive words or phrases?
- [ ] Do sentence lengths vary?
- [ ] Is it engaging?

## Final Pass: Polish

- [ ] Spelling and grammar
- [ ] Punctuation
- [ ] Formatting consistency
- [ ] Citations and links
- [ ] Read aloud for flow
