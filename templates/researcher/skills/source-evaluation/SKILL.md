# Source Evaluation

## CRAAP Test

### Currency
- When was it published/updated?
- Is the information current for your topic?
- Are links functional?

### Relevance
- Does it relate to your research question?
- Who is the intended audience?
- Is the information at an appropriate level?

### Authority
- Who is the author/publisher?
- What are their credentials?
- Is there contact information?
- What is the domain (.edu, .gov, .com)?

### Accuracy
- Where does the information come from?
- Is it supported by evidence?
- Can you verify it elsewhere?
- Is it peer-reviewed?

### Purpose
- Why does this information exist?
- Is it fact, opinion, or propaganda?
- Are there biases?
- Is it trying to sell something?

## Red Flags

- No author or publication date
- Emotional language without evidence
- Claims that can't be verified
- Single-source claims on important facts
- Obvious commercial motivation
